Game Design

Cowboy Hates Cockroaches

The game is made with Adobe Flash C65 Professional since 2013 with my game design team "Alliance".

Try the game here.

The Design

Menu

We designed the menu with a background of broken particles.
The menu consists of the following:
-The play button
-instructions/game controls
-game credits for the creators

Instructions

The instruction consists of the following:
-Rotation of mouse to move the cowboy.
-W,A,S,D to move.
-Left mouse-click to shoot.
-Press 1 to fire laser.
-Press 2 to use shield.
-Press 3 to use bomb.
Overall, your job is not to let the cockroaches come inside the house.

Levels

After clicking on play, you have 5 choices, either going in from
the first level, or choose a level anywhere from the 5 choices.
The 5 levels:
-Winter (1st level)
-Spring (2nd level)
-Summer (3rd level)
-Fall (4th level)
-Boss (final level)
The cockroaches get faster and stronger in each level and will
attack with the Boss in the final one.

Snapshots

From the first, level 1 and the final stage.

Scoring

Stats will show after finishing a level.

Introduction

Before the game starts, a comic strip appears showing the story of the
main character.
The user has an option to skip this intro by pressing the lower right 'skip'
button of the comic strip.
