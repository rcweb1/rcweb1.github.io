Website Usability Test

ICI UI/UX Design Usability Test

Iligan Computer Institute (ICI) is a technical school in the Philippines which has 3 campuses. My colleagues and I decided to volunteer for a Usability Test. So we broke our goals to ensure the usability test will be effective with a test goal and a problem statement.

Visit ICI.

Test Goals and Objectives

The usability test on the website of the Iligan Computer Institute was conducted with the following goals:

  • To evaluate the user’s ease of understanding of the website’s navigation and identifying concerns regarding the design of its user interface.
  • To locate errors and ambiguities on the presentation of information desired by the website’s users.
  • To provide possible solutions and recommendations to address issues discovered from the information collected.

Problem Statement:

The following questions will be the focus of this usability test:

  • Can the users easily find the information that they need?
  • Can the users easily find their way and navigate through the website?
  • Is the website’s presentation of information clear to the users?
  • Are the information provided by the website with regards to the school and other related matters reliable and complete?

So the test was conducted with 12 randomly selected graduating high school students, all of which who have decided and undecided to pursue their careers.
